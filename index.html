Concerned To Go Out: A Response Tool for COVID-19

"Concerned To Go Out" is a tool built by web developers based in Boston.
This website's purpose is designed to connect those willing to travel and deliver items
during the COVID-19 outbreak to those who are concerned to go out, due to either physical or mental limitations.
